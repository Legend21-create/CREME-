Our Story

The Science of Sweetness

At Creme Theory, we believe baking is the perfect alchemy of precision, passion, and premium ingredients. Based in Jamaica, we craft refined sweets for those who appreciate the finer details.

Our Philosophy

Founded on the principle that every bite should be an experience, Creme Theory moves away from mass production. We specialise in small-batch artisanal pastries and custom-designed cakes.

From the flakiest croissants to multi-tiered celebration masterpieces, our process remains the same: No shortcuts. No compromises.

Every order is made to order, every layer folded by hand, every detail considered. That's what separates a Creme Theory creation from everything else.
