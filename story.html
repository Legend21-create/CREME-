The Science of Sweetness

At Creme Theory, we believe baking is the perfect alchemy of precision, passion, and premium ingredients. Based in Jamaica, we craft refined sweets for those who appreciate the finer details.

Our Philosophy

Founded on the principle that every bite should be an experience, Creme Theory moves away from mass production. We specialize in small-batch artisanal pastries and custom-designed cakes.

From the flakiest croissants to multi-tiered wedding masterpieces, our process remains the same: No shortcuts. No compromises.
