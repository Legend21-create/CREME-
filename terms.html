Terms of Service

Effective February 2026

1. Custom Orders & Deposits

All custom orders require a 50% non-refundable deposit to secure your date. Your order is not confirmed until the deposit has been received and acknowledged by Creme Theory.

2. Final Payments

Remaining balances for wedding cakes and large events are due 7 days prior to the event date.

For Store Pickups:

Customers must either send 100% of the payment via Bank Transfer prior to arrival or pay the remaining balance in Cash at the time of pickup.

3. Changes & Cancellations

Changes to design or flavor must be requested at least 14 days before your event. While we strive to accommodate changes, additional fees may apply. Cancellations made within 48 hours of the event will be charged the full order amount.

4. Delivery & Pickups

Once an order has been picked up or delivered and signed for, Creme Theory is no longer responsible for any damage caused by improper transport or storage. We recommend keeping all cream-based products refrigerated until 1 hour before serving.

5. Dietary Information

Our kitchen handles nuts, gluten, and dairy. While we offer "friendly" options (e.g., gluten-free), we cannot guarantee a 100% allergen-free environment due to the nature of our artisanal process.
